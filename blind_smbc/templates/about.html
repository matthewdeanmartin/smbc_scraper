About This Project

SMBC for the Blind is a specialized accessibility project designed to provide a high-efficiency, text-based interface for the comic Saturday Morning Breakfast Cereal.

This is an LLM-assisted transformation of official SMBC comics. The process of generating transcripts and descriptions was performed by various Large Language Models.

Matthew Martin supervised the LLMs to ensure accuracy and maintain the spirit of the original work.

Accessibility Features

  • High-contrast dark mode.
  • Smart focus management for rapid Tab/Enter navigation.
  • Semantic HTML5 for screen reader efficiency.
  • No conflicting access keys.
